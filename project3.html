Project Overview

We received the analysis and design report of a competition platform application. Based on this document we were tasked to make the application in a group of other students. The applications had to be made in Laravel. I volunteered to be scrum master, so I was in charge of managing the sprints, the backlog and keeping the entire team updated on progress and tasks. Some of the functions of the application are; applying for a competition, submitting a file, voting for a winner and creating your own competitions.

Learning & Approach

An additional task I took up was being scrum leader. I made sure we held stand-up meetings, assigned tasks to everyone and constantly updated the jira dashboard. I supervised the creation of the project and steered everyone in right direction. It is sometimes not easy, since opinions can differ and small conflicts can occur. The team leader also needs to be on top of the requirements of the project, so when unnecessary conflict does occur, there is an authoritative voice to clear up the confusion.
